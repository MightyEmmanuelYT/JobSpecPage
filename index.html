Job Specification

Web Developer

Job Summary

You will be in charge of planning, creating, and maintaining websites for our company in your capacity as a website developer. You will collaborate closely with our team to comprehend project specifications and transform them into websites that are both practical and aesthetically beautiful. You'll be expected to create bespoke websites that satisfy the requirements of our clients using your knowledge of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and content management platforms like WordPress. You will also be in charge of making sure that every website is optimised for speed, functionality, and search engine optimisation (SEO). To provide high-quality work on schedule and under budget, you should be at ease working individually and cooperating with cross-functional teams. As a website developer, you will also be expected to keep up of new technologies and trends in web development and to incorporate them into our projects as necessary. The ideal candidate will have a bachelor's degree in computer science or a closely connected discipline and appropriate professional experience.

Job Requirements

To be considered for the role of website developer, you should possess a strong foundation in web development principles and have experience designing and developing websites from scratch. You should have a deep understanding of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and content management systems such as WordPress. You should be proficient in responsive design techniques and have a strong eye for detail to ensure that websites look great on all devices. Additionally, you should have experience with website optimization techniques, including performance, speed, and SEO. You should be able to work independently and as part of a team, communicating effectively with non-technical stakeholders. You should be comfortable working in an agile environment and have experience working with cross-functional teams. A bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related field is preferred, along with relevant professional experience. Demonstrated experience working on successful web development projects is a must-have. Experience with other programming languages such as PHP, Python, or Ruby is a plus.

 

Average yearly pay = 19k-25k for junior-level, 25k-35k for mid-level developer and 35k-75k for high-level developers

Web Designer

Job Summary

You will be in charge of producing aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly websites for our company in your capacity as a web designer. You will collaborate closely with our team to comprehend project requirements and create website designs that satisfy the expectations of our clients while upholding brand coherence. In order to improve the user experience, you will be in charge of designing website layouts, picking suitable colour schemes, and producing images and other visual elements. Additionally, it will be your responsibility to guarantee that website designs are SEO- and speed-optimized. To provide high-quality work on schedule and under budget, you should be at ease working individually and cooperating with cross-functional teams. You will also be expected as a web designer to keep up of new developments in web design trends and technology and to incorporate them into our projects as necessary. Along with relevant professional experience, a bachelor's degree in graphic design, web design, or a related field is preferred.

Job Requirements

You must have an impressive portfolio of accomplished website designs that demonstrates your capacity to produce aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly websites in order to be given consideration for the position of web designer. You should be an adept in graphic design programmes like Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator and have a thorough understanding of web design principles. To effectively explain your ideas, you need be skilled at making wireframes, prototypes, and other design artefacts. To make your designs a reality, you should also be able to collaborate with other designers, developers, and non-technical stakeholders. You ought to feel at ease in an agile setting and have previous experience leading cross-functional teams. Along with relevant professional experience, a bachelor's degree in graphic design, web design, or a related field is preferred. You should have prior expertise creating websites that are fast, effective, and SEO-friendly. A requirement is having experience working on successful web design projects. A plus is having knowledge of front-end web development technologies including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Your average yearly pay = 25k for entry-level and 42.5k for experienced workers
